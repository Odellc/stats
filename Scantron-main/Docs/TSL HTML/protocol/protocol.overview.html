Technology Solutions UK Ltd ASCII Protocol

Overview

All Technology Solutions UK Ltd (TSL) UHF RFID readers support the ASCII Protocol. This is a text, and line, based protocol that is designed to be easy to read by humans and also simple to parse by a machine.

Commands are sent as a single line to a reader and one or more lines are sent in response to the command.
